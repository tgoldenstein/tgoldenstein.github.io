Well, hello! I'm Taylor Goldenstein. I'm a recent journalism school grad from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, and I am am currently an intern in the metro department at the Chicago Tribune. I am a general assignment reporter and learning tons.

Before coming to the Tribune, I was fortunate enough to be a visiting fellow at the Nieman Foundation for Journalism at Harvard, where I worked on building a site for college journalists. Stay tuned for more on that!

When I don't have my nose in my notes, you can find me playing guitar or web hunting, accidentally-three-hours-into-research-on-a-new-story-idea-I-probably-don't-have-time-for-but-omg-wouldn't-it-be-so-great.

Above all, I want to help others make sense of what's going on around them, and boy, what an exciting crossroad journalism is at today in enhancing that experience for you, dear reader. You just wait.
