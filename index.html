Well, hello! I'm Taylor Goldenstein. I am a data journalist at the Los Angeles Times on the Data Desk.


As of late, I've been specializing in data-driven journalism, but really, I'm a fan of keeping the adjectives out of our titles. We do it all now, or that's what we're striving for at least. Why? Because what's most important is finding the most compelling and gripping way to tell stories that matter.


So call me what you like. Above all, I want to help others make sense of what's going on around them, and boy, what an exciting crossroad journalism is at today in enhancing that experience for you, dear reader. You just wait.
