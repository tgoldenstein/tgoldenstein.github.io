Well, hello! I'm Taylor Goldenstein. I'm a recent journalism school grad from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign and currently a visiting fellow at the Nieman Foundation for Journalism at Harvard, where I am working on building a site for college journalists.

This spring I will be the metro intern at the Chicago Tribune, and this summer, I will be a news intern at the Tampa Bay Times.

I feel incredibly fortunate that I will not need the #postgradproblems hashtag any time soon.

When I don't have my nose in my notes, you can find me playing guitar or web hunting, accidentally-three-hours-into-research-on-a-new-story-idea-I-probably-don't-have-time-for-but-omg-wouldn't-it-be-so-great.

Above all, I want to help others make sense of what's going on around them, and boy, what an exciting crossroad journalism is at today in enhancing that experience for you, dear reader. You just wait.
